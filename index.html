About Us

Established in 1998, OT Construction has been a leading specialist in plastering services with over 20 years of experience in the residential construction industry. Our expertise extends beyond plastering; we undertake a wide range of building and renovation projects, ensuring high-quality results on every job.

Our dedicated team delivers exceptional craftsmanship, no matter the size or complexity of the task. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Over the years, we've built a solid reputation for our meticulous attention to detail and our ability to complete projects on time and within budget.

We serve homeowners all over the UK, providing personalized services tailored to meet the unique needs of each project. From interior refurbishments to complete home renovations, OT Construction is your trusted partner for all your residential construction needs.

Contact us today for a no-obligation quote and discover how our experienced team can bring your vision to life. Whether you're looking to refurbish your home, develop a new extension, or require specialist plastering, OT Construction has the skills and experience to deliver outstanding results.
